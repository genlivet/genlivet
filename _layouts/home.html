cooperative

Genlivet is a group of multidisciplinary
humans working together on regenerative
solutions, with global implications, locally.

We are organized equitably and distribute
all the value we generate fairly among
ourselves and all the local actors we
engage.

Equity: Equal compensation for labor
adjusted by the local purchasing power.

SOLUTIONS

Genlivet takes the boots on the ground,
grassroots approach to engage and activate local
actors to find and execute regenerative solutions
to their local waste problems.

Regenerative: Is a process originated by an initial investment of resources,
labor and energy that generates, as an output, as many necessary inputs
as needed to sustain itself.

APPROACH

We strive to use only waste materials, identify local waste stream and sources, and redirect or reduce them by providing alternatives, in favor of a circular economy system.

Circular Economy System: is an economic system aimed at eliminating waste and the continual use of resources.
