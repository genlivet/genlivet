Equity:

Equal compensation for labor adjusted by the local purchasing power.

_Who we are

We are organized equitably and distribute all the value we generate fairly among ourselves and all the local actors we engage.

_What we do

Genlivet takes the boots on the ground, grassroots approach to engage and activate local actors to find and execute regenerative solutions to their local waste problems.

Regenerative:

Is a process originated by an initial investment of resources, labor and energy that generates, as an output, as many necessary inputs as needed to sustain itself.

Circular Economy System:

is an economic system aimed at eliminating waste and the continual use of resources.

_How we do it

We strive to use only waste materials, identify local waste stream and sources, and redirect or reduce them by providing alternatives, in favor of a circular economy system.
